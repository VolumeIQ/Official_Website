FAQ

Common Questions

Is VolumeIQ free?
Yes, completely free with all features. Optional patron themes support development but are purely cosmetic.
Does it collect my data?
No. Zero analytics, zero tracking, zero servers. All data stays in your browser's local storage and optional Chrome Sync.
Will boosting above 100% damage speakers?
The boost amplifies the browser's audio signal, not system volume. Use the Safe Volume feature to set a cap for hearing protection.
Does it work on Netflix / Spotify?
DRM sites work at up to 100%. Boost, normalization require non-DRM audio. Per-site memory, mute, and mixer work on all sites.
How is this different from other volume boosters?
Most boosters just amplify. VolumeIQ offers per-tab mixing, normalization, per-site memory, smart ducking, keyboard shortcuts, and a premium UI - all with zero data collection.
Can I export / import settings?
Yes. Settings → Data → Export downloads a JSON file. Import on another device to restore everything.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

Overview

VolumeIQ is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the Extension accesses and how it's used.

Data Collection

VolumeIQ collects zero personal data.

  • No analytics or usage data
  • No cookies or tracking
  • No external server communication
  • No browsing history access beyond active tab hostname
  • No personally identifiable information

Data Stored Locally

Stored locally using Chrome storage APIs:

  • Site preferences: hostname + volume level
  • Settings: normalization, ducking, theme, presets
  • Session state: per-tab volume/mute (cleared on tab close)

Chrome Sync may sync settings across devices through your Google account. VolumeIQ has no access to your Google account.

Permissions

  • "Access data on all websites" - Injects audio processing script. Only interacts with <audio>/<video> elements.
  • "tabs" - Tab Mixer feature.
  • "storage" - Save preferences.
  • "favicon" - Display site icons.

Third-Party Services

Zero third-party services, SDKs, or analytics. No ads, no telemetry.

Data Deletion

Delete all data: Settings → Data → "Reset All Data", or uninstall the Extension.
